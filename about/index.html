Troy Budd

Hi, I'm Troy, an enthusiastic and driven Front-End Web Developer from London. After recently returning from a long trip away, I set my focus on becoming a Front-End Web developer. After a few months of learning, I gained a unique opportunity to secure a scholarship paid for by Google, for a Udacity Nanodegree.

The challenge entailed 3 months of completing a practice course with a final end project, as well as participating regularly on the forums and slack, offering help when possible. After 3 months of hard work, I got the email detailing how my hard work had shown, and that I had been chosen as the top 3% out of 200,000 people to gain the scholarship!

One year on from when I began, I have in-depth knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript (JavaScript being my focus). Now I have great foundations in JavaScript I am turning my attention towards React to improve my current thinking methodologies aswell as learn the industry standard.
